A birthday, to me, might mean more than just a celebration. It’s a personal milestone. A moment to pause and reflect on how far I’ve come, where I am now, and what’s ahead. It’s a day to honor my growth, both the challenges and the triumphs, and to appreciate the person I am becoming. Birthdays are like checkpoints in your life journey, reminding me to stay grounded in simplicity and truth, while still dreaming big for the future. It’s also a chance to reconnect with myself, appreciate the little things, and set new intentions for the year ahead.
