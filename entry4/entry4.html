Today is my first birthday,
A festival blooming with my parents' love.
The sweetness of a soft cake wraps around my tongue,
As laughter blossoms among my family gathered near.

In a harmony of love,
They sing my name, blessing me with joy,
And to their warm melodies,
I wave my tiny hands in delight.

Their gazes sparkle like starlight,
May this precious moment linger forever,
Today, I stand at the heart of love,
Wrapped in happiness,
My spirit swells with pure delight!
